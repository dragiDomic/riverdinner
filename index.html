About us..

Served food every day since 1997

River Diner opened its doors on a crisp autumn day in 1997. Chef and owner Marko Petrovic began by crafting exquisite dishes and sharing them with local restaurants and his neighbors from a cozy kitchen near the serene banks of the Dunav River in Belgrade. Today, Serbia's cherished diner and culinary gem celebrates over 25 years of serving classic, made-from-scratch Balkan and international cuisine.
